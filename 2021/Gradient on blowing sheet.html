ABOUT:

A projection installation of an artificial gradient map projected onto a translucent sheet blowing in the wind.


Another variation of this experiment projected artificial edge maps onto multiple translucent sheets blowing in the wind that periodically got wiped out by a bright flash.


TECHNICAL INFO:

This system was controlled by a custom program made in Touchdesigner that generated the gradient maps as live video and sent DMX signal over ethernet via ArtNET to the lights.
