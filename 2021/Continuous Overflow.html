Continuous Overflow

December 2021

A projection installation of two projectors projecting slow circular gradients at each other with 3 layers of translucent sheets in between.

ABOUT:

This piece was made in the class Vision in Art and Neuroscience in the Brain and Cognitive Sciences Department and was shown in the MIT Compton Gallery as part of the group show ‘Emissive’.


EXHIBITION TEXT:

Circle expanding. Sphere emerging. Circle receding. Sheet swaying. Sphere disappearing. Sheet wrinkling. Circle expanding.


TECHNICAL INFO:

The animation is generated live off of a continuous noise function with time being the seed in a custom program made in Touchdesigner.
