ABOUT:

Trailer I made for MIT: REGRESSIONS, a documentary by MIT students Luke Igel and Wesley Block following the history of the Institute spanning from world War 2 to COVID-19.


MIT has long promised to engineer a better world. This film asks a simple question: Have we kept our promise?
