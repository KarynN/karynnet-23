My Canon TS 6030 sucks!

January 2022

50 postcards created by forcing variation through iterating on printer error via misuse.

ABOUT:

I forced a Canon TS 6030 printer to print on smaller pieces of paper than allowed, causing it to miss the center and spit out the paper at a semi random slant and position depending on however the paper lifting mechanism happens and how well I initially center the paper in the tray. I experimented with this on 50 variations of grids, lines, shapes, and the words “blur” and 「ぶれ」(blur in Japanese).
